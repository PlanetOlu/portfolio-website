About Me

Hi there! I'm Abimbola Oluwamodupe, a creative technologist based in Lagos, Nigeria, with a passion for turning complex ideas into clean interfaces, clear words, and compelling visuals. With a background in Mass Communication and a multidisciplinary skill set spanning front-end development, UI/UX design, technical copywriting, video editing and graphic design.
I help startups and businesses bring their products to life—faster, smarter, and with more impact.

My journey has been fueled by relentless curiosity and a love for both storytelling and technology. I’ve had the privilege of working with brands like Tomike Couture, House of Tara, EVVDEE, CEMCS, Jui City and a few others, contributing to projects that ranged from digital platforms to creative campaigns.

Whether I’m designing a dashboard, writing onboarding copy, or editing a product video, I bring clarity, creativity, and precision to every project. I believe the best solutions are crafted through empathy, collaboration, and a deep understanding of user needs.
